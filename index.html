About

Norlan Prudente graduated in 2018 of December. He has attained both of his Bachelor's and Master's Degree at the University of the Pacific, through Blended Master's program, in Computer Science with concentraion on Software Development/Engineering. He is currently working as a Senior System Engineer at Form Factor in Livermore California.

His main role is automotion, data gathering, data verification, data analysis, software development, software optimization and technical support on in-house tools/programs. He is currently using C# and VBA to develop programs along with the combinaion of excel chart and formula to deliver software requirements. Some of his notable skills are optimizing programs, through usage of data structure and parallelism, which are more noticeable the larger the data gets. The speed gained are at least 3x faster. Additionally, he prepares Cycle Time Reduction and First Pass Yield Metrics for presentation that managers and director uses.

He also has experienced with his previous internship, 8 months in Vocera, with an agile software development environment and technical support skills. In addition, he has 5 years of experience in tutoring computer science classes. Lastly, he worked as an IT tech specialist in the Eberhardt School of Business IT department for 5 months.

Throughout his school year, he has worked on multiple projects both individually and as a team member. He has held multiple lead position while working with a team such as lead programmer, group leader that assigned task, design lead, and evaluator lead. His preferred computer language is Python 3, but are capable of using other languages such as C, C++, Java, Javascript, Html, CSS, and PHP. He has knowledge of different programming language paradigm: imperative, functional, logical, and object oriented. He also has basic knowledge towards AI, Machine Learning, and Robotics.
